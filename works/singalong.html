Development Process

First Challenge

Being the first & only Designer in the team

The CEO recruited me as an app developer at first, but after an interview, he decided to hire me as a UX designer.

However, he’s not sure what I can do for them, as their product isalready designed and is under a development process, but heexpected me to find some usability issues in the app to improve.

Since the app is already designed by the product lead, I also need tocommunicate with him a lot to convince him to change the design.
What I did:

• Understand the market thoroughly by downloading all-related apps and analysed their usability

• Sit the with decision makers (CEO, Product Lead) and ask them toprioritise our design needs by mapping the CJM.

• Gain trust: Interview with different roles to understand everyone’sexpectation on the product and their priorities

• Do more user researches to convince managers.

Being the first designer in the team, I believe I am also responsible for bringing that UX mindset to the team. Second Challenge

I don't speak Swedish...

I started interviewing a few users of our product, but I found out since most of our beta-users are teenagers, and not very proficient in speaking English, it’s hard to find out their thoughts and feedbacks.

Same challenge when I design the survey as most of them couldn’t understand english.
What I did:

• Don’t do interviews only, observe them using the app first and then note down their actions, then ask about why they did that afterwards.
‍
• Survey are translated to Swedish by my colleague, some of the results are in Swedish as well and I have to talk with my colleagues again to understand what they actually means.

Design with limitations…

Base on the persona, I prioritised a few directions that I can do to improve the app:
